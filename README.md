## About Portman Lights

Portman® Lights designs and manufactures professional stage lighting with iconic designs recognized around the world. Since 2016, this Polish company has created beautiful, unique, and timeless fixtures that combine artistic vision with cutting-edge technology, distributed across 56+ countries with a portfolio of spectacular concerts and TV shows.

### The Portman Story
Founded in 2016 by Dominik and Łukasz, Portman® Lights emerged from years of development - the first product concepts were drawn in 2012. This perfect combination of business expertise (Dominik) and technical experience (Łukasz, a former lighting designer with electronic studies background) created a company focused on delivering what the industry truly needs.

### Polish Design Heritage
**Made in Poland** - All designs, prototypes, and final products are created in-house. Most components are manufactured in Poland on Portman's own assembly line, ensuring complete quality control and authentic Polish craftsmanship.

### Strategic Partnership
Since 2019, Portman has been supported by the "French Team" - original founders of Ayrton. This combination brings together Polish innovation with established European lighting industry experience, creating "a perfect combination of dynamism and stability, visionary and experience."
